About Me

I have been a tutor in GCSE Mathematics and Science for over 20 years, and more recently, I have tutored the A-Level Biology syllabus.

I have a proven track record of improving students' grades and am passionate about my subject, striving to bring out the best in every student.

My teaching style is encouraging and hands-on, with a strong focus on subject knowledge and exam techniques. I aim to provide a positive learning experience while helping students reach their full potential.

I have also worked in the Maths and Science departments of various secondary schools in London. I offer both online and in-person tutoring in Farnham, nearby areas, and London.

Since 2020, during the COVID period, I adapted my teaching to help students succeed through online learning and effectively prepare for their exams during this challenging time.

Currently, I offer both online and in-person tutoring. My online lessons have successfully helped students improve, with some finding this approach particularly beneficial in saving travel time and enhancing flexibility.

I have worked with students from both state and private schools, primarily in London, and have adapted my teaching style to meet their individual needs.

I am fully conversant with the requirements for GCSE Science (Biology, Chemistry, and Physics) and Mathematics across all examination boards, as well as the A-Level Biology syllabus.

Finally, I have a nurturing and focused teaching style and look forward to working with you. I will track your progress continuously and support you in achieving your target grades and hope to get the best out of you.
